This is a demonstration of flowchart connectors and mouse events.

Hover over connections to highlight them, click to delete.

Drag new connections from blue dots to green dots.

This demonstration uses jsPlumb 1.3.3, jQuery 1.6.0 and jQuery UI 1.8.13.
