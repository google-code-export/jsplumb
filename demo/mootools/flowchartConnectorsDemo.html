This is a demonstration of Flowchart connectors and mouse events.

Hover over connections to highlight them, click to delete.

Drag new connections from blue dots to green dots. You can also drag connections from green dots onto other green dots or back onto themselves.

The blue endpoints on this page are fixed to the BottomCenter anchor location, but the green ones switch from LeftMiddle to RightMiddle depending on the position of the other element in the connection.

This demonstration uses jsPlumb 1.3.5, MooTools 1.3.2 and MooTools More 1.3.2.1.
